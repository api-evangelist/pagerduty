<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The agent connects directly to PagerDuty’s event intelligence, on-call data, and service context. <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">And because it operates as a virtual responder within your existing workflows, the SRE Agent participates right alongside you in Slack, Microsoft Teams, PagerDuty&#8217;s Operations Console, or Incident Details Page (now available for <a href="https://pagerduty.com/early-access">Early Access</a>), suggesting remediations from your runbooks, and even executing predefined actions when authorized.</span></p>

<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Collaboration setup:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Creates or joins incident channels automatically</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Incident summarization:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Maintains a rolling timeline of key events for stakeholders</span></li>
