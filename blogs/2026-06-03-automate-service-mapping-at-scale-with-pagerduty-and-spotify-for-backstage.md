PagerDuty has released automated service mapping capabilities for its Spotify for Backstage plugin, now generally available. The feature uses an intelligent matching algorithm to automatically connect PagerDuty services to Backstage components, reducing manual configuration work and enabling teams to achieve complete service catalogs more efficiently while maintaining accurate incident routing.
